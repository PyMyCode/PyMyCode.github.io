Buying Process

  • Do research and find a home
  • Go to banks to understand the mortgage options
  • Make an offer
  • If offer is accepted, reservation fee of 0.5-1% (non refundatable). Property taken off the market for about 4 weeks
  • Submit relevant documents to the Banks
  • 10 days to process the loans and finalize
  • Transaction needs to be Notarized at a Notary
  • Some weeks later, the notary will set a date for you to transfer the full purchase amount to the seller. You’ll transfer the money to cover your down payment, and the mortgage lender will transfer the remainder
  • Get the keys
  • Entry in land registery..official ownership
  • you should have received your transfer tax bill, which must be paid within four weeks
